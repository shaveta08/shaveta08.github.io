Max Temprature


As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches equator (0 Deg. Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of the year than northern hemisphere. This may be sure to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
